One-sided cards

An Anki2 add-on

When the back side of a card is exactly {{FrontSide}}, go directly to the back side. This can be used for cards that describe a task. Like this, you save the time of clicking on the Show Answer button.

This add-on works somewhat, but there may be a few problems with the first card. I don't use this, so i will not spend too much time fixing the problem.
