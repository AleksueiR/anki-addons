Quick replay

An Anki2 add-on

Play mp3 files with mpg321, and flac and ogg files with play (that is, sox). This bypasses the mplayer play queue. Like this, when you hit replay, the replay starts at once.

Some versions of sox for Windows may not be able to play sound. In this case, you may want to uninstall this add-on.

The add-on checks if mpg321 and play are installed, but does nothing to fix it if they are not there.

The advantage over the Play-with-mpgNN and Play with ogg add-ons is that this plays all sound files of a card one after the other for each play or replay, avoiding the ugly situation that several files are played at once.
