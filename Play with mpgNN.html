Play with mpg123

An Anki2 add-on

This is a quick hack to play mp3 files with a dedicated mp3 player, rather than with mplayer. This intentionally bypasses the mplayer play queue. The advantage is that replaying the media starts immediately, even before the first playing is finished.

The disadvantage is that when a card contains several audio files, they are played simultaneously, which is usually not what we want. The Quick replay add-on should fix this problem.
