Anki 1.2 shortcuts

An Anki2 add-on

Reactivate three Anki 1 shortcuts:

  • F5 replay audio
  • Ctr+f card browser
  • Ctrl+w Deck overview

This is just a little rework of the oldshortcuts.py add-on, dropping Ctr+d for add card and adding F5 for replay audio.
